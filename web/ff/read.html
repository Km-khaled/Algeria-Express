Counted among the world’s largest rail networks, Algeria Railways is the most popular mode of transport for passengers. Rail way is the leading portal for making your IRCTC ticket booking within a few minutes. All you have to do is enter your source and destination and look at all the trains available on the selected route. In order to confirm your tickets, enter your IRCTC login ID and be ready to embark on a journey of a lifetime. You can also book your train tickets on MakeMyTrip’s mobile app. Once you are done booking your ticket, you will receive a confirmation message on your mobile. Moreover, you would receive your e-train ticket on the mentioned email ID. Instead of carrying physical tickets, all you have to do is show your e-train ticket received on your mobile to the ticket collector.

The development of the tram network in Algeria requires perfect mastery of the skills acquired during the transfer of know-how from the experts of the RATP group to the employees of Rail way. These skills are necessary to guarantee the success of these projects at each stage, in particular, the design, commissioning and development of urban rail transport. In order to be part of continuous progress, a training center has been dedicated since May 2014, bringing together several training sessions focusing mainly on rail safety and support professions.

The main objective is to maintain and guarantee over time the levels of safety, availability and reliability of the transport system in order to provide passengers with a public service in compliance with AFNOR standards, safety requirements, regularity and of quality. Thus the main missions are: Carry out maintenance operations (preventive and corrective) of systems (tracks, ticketing, contact wire, power supply, radio, telephony, passenger information, etc.) and structures dedicated to the tram, as well as the good state of cleanliness . Manage the actions and purchases of spare parts thanks to the CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management). Develop the technical reference system and the related training.
